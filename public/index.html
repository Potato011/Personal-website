Long Endurance Fixed Wing Camera Platform

A autonomous, long endurance, high altitude camera platform for gimballed fpv Overview I planned out this aircraft to solely focus on efficiency and stable flight performance. With a 5.5Kg max takeoff weight and two 6s 10aH lipo batteries, this aircraft can stay in the air for over 3 hours. Swap one of the batteries for a downward facing DSLR camera and the aircraft becomes a capable mapping platform. Control is handled via the latest H7 processor running arduplane firmware enabling the aircraft to navigate autonomously, accept waypoint missions, and operate a three axis gimbal installed in the nose....
